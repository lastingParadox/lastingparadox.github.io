About Me

Hi! I'm a third year Computer Science student at the University of Central Florida who will be graduating sometime in Spring 2024. While my projects are rather varied in scope, I currently am working as a web developer for UCF's Center of Distributed Learning's Techrangers. At my workplace, I develop and maintain web applications to enhance the online learning experience for students and faculty, while learning to work in a professional team environment. In addition, I am also an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant for the College of Engineering and Computer Science. As a ULA, I teach rising Computer Science students core concepts of software development, particularly Object-Oriented Programming. I characterize myself as a curious person, typically digging into many things at once while working on projects. Adding in the meticulous aspects of sci-fi worldbuilding and writing, I like to diversify my productivity from time to time. Also, I enjoy watching movies, particularly silly horror films and films with imaginative concepts behind them.
